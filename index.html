Welcome to Escape to Break

Congratulations! You've just finished the last bit of work at Baldwin, and the holiday break is around the corner.

Word has spread about a mysterious box hidden in the school that holds something extraordinary.

Excited about the adventure that lies ahead, you and your friends decide to embark on a quest to find and unlock the box before leaving for your holiday break. Choose a few friends to work with to try to find the box, open the box, and see what's inside!
