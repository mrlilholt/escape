An Encounter with a Magical Portal

Well, you are out of the basement... but now you stand before a magical portal, adorned with mystical symbols. To activate it, perform the following actions in the correct order:

  1. Touch the ancient tome on the pedestal.
  2. Ring the enchanted bell hanging from the ceiling.
  3. Rotate the ornate key in the mysterious lock.
  4. Light the candles in the correct order: left, right, center.
